Ports at the Partition Boundary

MSIE is supported for SystemVerilog, traditional Verilog, and VHDL. This means that the place where the design is split during the elaboration and reconnected at the beginning of the simulation must be written in specific languages. Additionally, any combination of these languages is supported at the partition boundary: Verilog-Verilog, VHDL-VHDL, SystemVerilog-Verilog, SystemVerilog-VHDL, and mixed Verilog/VHDL. Lower levels can include The e language or SystemC.

The partitions can communicate through port connections at the partition boundary. This allows each partition to contribute drivers and fanout for the same net.
